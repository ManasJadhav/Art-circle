OBJECTIVES

  • To refine colloquial and written forms of communication.
  • To generate effective critical thinking about primary issues in a given topic.
  • To instill confidence for presenting a person’s views on a given topic at a public platform.
  • To provide a significant training ground for the development of students’ communication abilities.
  • To provide a platform for students to understand and communicate various forms or arguments effectively in a variety of contexts.

ABOUT US

Express your opinions through

PICT DEBSOC is a community that develops and nurtures public speaking skills and a variety of soft skills which are essential in today’s world. The modern world requires people to not only be technically proficient in their work but also be outspoken leaders who can initiate a change. We ensure that students improve their oratory skills by giving them a platform where they can voice their opinions and build their confidence.

  • Do you want to be a great leader?
  • Do you want to ace your placement interviews?
  • Do you want to be an effective communicator and team player?

PICT DEBSOC is the place to learn it all.

OUR COMMITTEES

We believe in teamwork for achieving our collective objectives.

Internal Affairs Committee

The Internal Affairs Committee(or IAC) is responsible for handling the internal operations of the club. It also organizes a range of intra-club competitions for our members and ensures active participation from everyone.

External Affairs Committee

The External Affairs Committee(or EAC) organizes and manages various intra-college as well as inter-college activities. It also collaborates with the debate societies of other colleges and hosts numerous events.

Content Team

The DEBSOC Content Team is group of like-minded people who are enthusiastic about writing and literature. The Content team writes content on various current issues or special occasions which is also posted on our social media handles.

Design Team

The DEBSOC Design Team is a group of enthusiastic artists who create beautiful posters and illustrations for all of our events and social media posts.

QUIZ CLUB

The Quiz Club is a group of like-minded enthusiasts who organize quiz competitions and represent the college at different quizzing events.

"It is better to debate a question without settling it than to settle a question without debating it."
―Joseph Joubert

Debating and putting forth your opinions confidently is the primary skill that we aim to develop at PICT DEBSOC. Debates are crucial to reach a conclusion in situations where there are two oppsosing points of views on a particular topic. Debates are regulary organized for the club members to polish their skills.

“Discussion can solve problems that guns and pills cannot.”
― Abhijit Naskar

Collaboration and discussion is crucial to the progress of any team, organization or society. Group discussions provide a conducive environment for thinking, brainstorming and and finding creative solutions to problems. They also serve as good preparation for campus interviews.

“You can speak well if your tongue can deliver the message of your heart.”
―John Ford

Speaking alone in front of an audience is many people's worst nightmare. Here at PICT DEBSOC, though, we practice this skill through extempore speeches on a wide range of topics. There is no better way to conquer one's fear of public speaking than through extempores.

"A word after a word after a word is power."
―Margaret Atwood

PICT DebSoc conducted SCRIBBLE IT DOWN, an online content writing competition, where the participants were required to write on the topic “Happiness”. The content allowed articles or poems but all under a 150-word limit. The week-long competition (26th July to 1st August) was open for all and received an overwhelming response with a total of 119 participants.

Unleash the power of the microphone

The Open Mic is a major event that is organized every year for students to showcase their skills in front of their peers. Whether through speech, poetry, music or art, the event is aimed at inspiring individuals to break the ice and express themselves freely. This year the Open Mic was held online(23rd and 24th July) on the topic 'Identity: the art that defines you'.

DSP is an initiative by PICT DEBSOC, where we invite eminent speakers and masters in the field of public speaking to share their knowledge and experiences exclusively with the staff and students of PICT.
